# Apex Habitat 0.8.3

This release lets you see your work on a calendar, move it across boards, and let it route
itself. No change to the room generator or other existing flows.

## What changed for users

- **See what's due on a calendar** — scheduled tasks (by due date), building licences (by
  expiry), service orders (by scheduled date) and leases (start to end) all laid out by
  date.
- **Turn rooms around faster** — a housekeeping board to drag each room from Needs
  Cleaning to Needs Repair to Ready, alongside the existing Resident and Maintenance
  boards.
- **Let requests route themselves** — new maintenance and resident requests can
  auto-assign to a team (load-balancing or round-robin). Shipped ready to switch on: add
  your real team and enable.
- **Keep your team in the loop** — opt-in email alerts for a maintenance request assigned
  to you, a new request awaiting triage, a resident request waiting on evidence, a new
  resident request, and a submitted custody damage assessment.

## What changed under the hood

- Auto-assignment and notifications arrive switched off, so an update never starts
  emailing or assigning until you've configured it.
- All new text is English-first and translated through `ar.csv`. The workspace layout is
  unchanged. Security & reliability improvements.

## If you are updating an existing site

Run `bench migrate` and clear cache after pulling. The new Kanban board, assignment rules
and notifications are seeded automatically (disabled). On the next Desk boot, Frappe shows
this update dialog once per user.
