# Apex Habitat 0.8.2

This release turns every building into its own store, with stock you can track and move,
and adds two drag-to-progress boards. No change to the room generator, maintenance, or
other existing flows.

## What changed for users

- **Every building is its own store** — issuing custody to an employee moves stock out of
  the building and into that employee's hands; returns move it back. It's all tracked on
  a new **Accommodation Stock Ledger**, so you always know what's where.
- **Send supplies between buildings with confidence** — a new **Accommodation Material
  Transfer** ships custody articles and maintenance materials from one building to
  another: submit ships the stock (In Transit), then **Mark Received** lands it in the
  destination. You can never move more than a store actually holds.
- **Keep finance in the picture** — when stock moves between buildings on different cost
  centres, finance can be emailed a heads-up memo so they can record the cross-charge.
- **Know what you have and what it's worth** — the new **Accommodation Stock Balance**
  report shows on-hand quantity and value for every store and every employee's custody,
  reachable from the Custody & Asset Control workspace.
- **Triage at a glance** — Resident Requests and Maintenance Requests now have
  drag-to-progress Kanban boards for daily work.

## What changed under the hood

- The stock ledger keeps balances correct and auditable, with full reversal on cancel.
- The cross-cost-centre memo is opt-in via Habitat Settings. Security & reliability
  improvements, with balance queries tuned for speed.

## If you are updating an existing site

Run `bench migrate` and clear cache after pulling. The Resident/Maintenance Kanban boards
are seeded automatically. On the next Desk boot, Frappe shows this update dialog once per
user.
