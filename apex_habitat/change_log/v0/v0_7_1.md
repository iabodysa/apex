# Apex Habitat 0.7.1

This patch turns on the official Frappe "Updated To A New Version" popup for the v0.7
upgrade and carries the v0.7 highlights below.

## What changed for users

- Build real buildings your way — the room generator now handles apartments, offices,
  storage, and driver, supervisor and worker rooms.
- Catch operational warnings early — scheduler alerts now surface in the desk.
- Turn a lease into a payment in one click, with a smoother finance review.
- Work in Arabic — translations and setup guidance were expanded for the new wizard and
  flows.
- Security & reliability improvements.

## What changed under the hood

- The app now ships official Frappe change-log files, so future updates can appear in the
  Desk update popup.

## If you are updating an existing site

Run bench migrate and clear cache after pulling this release. On the next Desk boot,
Frappe will read this change log and show the update dialog once per user.
