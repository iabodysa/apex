# Apex Habitat 0.8.1

This patch adds deep, in-context dashboards and fixes a form-dashboard bug.

## What changed for users

- **Building form dashboard** — opening an Accommodation Building now shows, at the
  top of the form, an occupancy-percent trend chart plus live indicators for
  active occupants, open maintenance, and open custody issues — alongside the
  related-records links (rooms, leases, licenses, occupancy snapshots).
- **Supplier form dashboard** — opening a Supplier now also shows their housing
  assignments, leases, subcontracting orders, and cost-recovery ledger entries,
  merged with the standard ERPNext supplier links (purchase orders, invoices).
- These dashboards build on the existing Workspace cards and charts to give a
  per-record view, not just a workspace-level one.

## What changed under the hood

- **Bug fix (security/stability):** the Employee form-dashboard override used an
  old signature and replaced — rather than merged with — the native ERPNext
  dashboard; it now merges, protecting core HR links. The Supplier override uses
  the same safe merge pattern.
- Dashboard metrics are read through a single permission-checked method, and the
  occupancy chart reuses the occupancy-snapshot engine for good performance (no
  recompute). No change to the room generator, maintenance, or other workflows.

## If you are updating an existing site

Run `bench migrate` and clear cache after pulling. On the next Desk boot, Frappe
shows this update dialog once per user.
