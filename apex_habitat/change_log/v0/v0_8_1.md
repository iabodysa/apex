# Apex Habitat 0.8.1

This patch adds rich, in-context dashboards to the Building and Supplier forms, plus
security and reliability improvements.

## What changed for users

- **Building form dashboard** — opening an Accommodation Building now greets you, right at
  the top, with an occupancy-percent trend chart and live indicators for active
  occupants, open maintenance and open custody issues, alongside its related records
  (rooms, leases, licences, occupancy snapshots).
- **Supplier form dashboard** — opening a Supplier now also shows their housing
  assignments, leases, subcontracting orders and cost-recovery entries, side by side with
  the standard ERPNext supplier links (purchase orders, invoices).
- Together these give you a per-record view, not just a workspace-level one.

## What changed under the hood

- Security & reliability improvements. No change to the room generator, maintenance, or
  other workflows.

## If you are updating an existing site

Run `bench migrate` and clear cache after pulling. On the next Desk boot, Frappe shows
this update dialog once per user.
