# Apex Habitat 0.9.1

This release brings guided setup to every workspace and shows what each record is
connected to. No change to the room generator, maintenance, or other flows.

## What changed for users

- **Guided setup, per workspace** — step-by-step banners walk you through getting started:
  - **Setup** → "Habitat Operations Setup" (configure settings, create site, building,
    rooms and beds, review the safety catalog, record an assignment).
  - **Accommodation** → "Accommodation Go-Live" (open the Front Desk, record an assignment,
    review the Active Resident Register).
  - **Safety** → "Safety Readiness" (review the safety catalog, log an inspection, open the
    Safety Map).
- **See every connection** — opening an **Accommodation Building** now shows its linked
  rooms, beds, assignments, requests, licences, custody, leases, utility and scheduling
  records, all grouped in the Connections tab. Accommodation Site and Accommodation Room
  get the same drill-down.

## What changed under the hood

- Onboarding is attached through the v15 workspace mechanism, pointing at real, verified
  setup steps (DocTypes, the Front Desk and Safety Map pages, and reports).
- Connections come from each record's own links, every one verified to resolve, so nothing
  renders broken. This is additive metadata only. Security & reliability improvements.

## If you are updating an existing site

Run `bench migrate` and clear cache after pulling. Ensure System Settings → "Enable
Onboarding" is on to see the setup banners. On the next Desk boot, Frappe shows this
update dialog once per user.
