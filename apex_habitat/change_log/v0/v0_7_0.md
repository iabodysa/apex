# Apex Habitat 0.7.0

This release makes building setup faster and more flexible, brings lease payments and
operational alerts into the desk, and ships broad security and reliability improvements.

## Accommodation and room setup

- Build a whole building in minutes with a more practical room generator that lays out
  floor after floor.
- Mix room types freely — worker, driver and supervisor rooms, offices, storage and
  maintenance rooms all in one plan.
- Read room and bed IDs at a glance with floor-code based numbering.
- Set up apartments as easily as buildings, with floor fields stepping out of the way
  when they don't apply.

## Operations, maintenance, and safety

- Catch operational warnings early — scheduler alerts now surface in the desk where
  you'll see them.
- Log every safety finding in one tidy list on the inspection report.
- Maintenance and scheduler flows are smoother and steadier.

## Finance and reporting

- Trust your cost figures — daily allocation now lands accurately every day of the year.
- Turn a lease into a payment in one click.
- A verified set of operational reports for housing, maintenance, safety and finance.

## Security and reliability

- Security & reliability improvements.
