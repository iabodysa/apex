# Apex 1.3.0

Salis is quicker to move around and quicker to act in, with a simpler set of roles
behind the scenes.

## What changed for users

- **Go straight to your area.** Salis is organised into clear areas — Workers Transport,
  Representatives Fleet, Fuel, Rentals & Costs, Compliance & Drivers and Setup — so you
  open the part you work in and start.
- **Create as you go.** Each area has one-tap create buttons — a new driver, vehicle,
  dispatch trip, fuel request or transport request — right where you need them.
- **A clearer setup area.** Masters like vehicle categories, rental offices, fuel
  platforms and settings now sit together under Setup.

## What changed for managers

- **A simpler role setup.** The Movement roles have been streamlined to a focused set
  that suits a small team while keeping approvals and oversight properly separated.

## Reliability

- Security & reliability improvements.

## If you are updating an existing site

Pull, then `bench migrate` and clear cache. Existing team members keep their access —
their roles are mapped to the streamlined set automatically.
