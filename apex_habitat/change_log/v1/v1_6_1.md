# Apex 1.6.1

A cleaner, more focused desk.

## What changed for users

- **The desk opens to your Apex areas.** Habitat, Salis and Apex Core stay front and
  centre; the underlying ERPNext module pages are hidden by default for everyday users
  so the sidebar isn't cluttered.
- **Fully reversible.** Hidden modules can be switched back on at any time — nothing is
  removed, only tidied out of the way.
- **Reliability improvements.**

## What changed under the hood

- A guarded, idempotent migration hides the stock ERPNext workspaces (by name, public
  only) and leaves all Apex and shared setup areas visible.

## If you are updating an existing site

Pull, then `bench migrate` and clear cache. Administrators with the Workspace Manager
role still see every workspace by design; everyday operational users see the focused
Apex desk.
