# Apex 1.7.0

Transport Requests now run on a real approval workflow, the reports answer more
questions, and the background engines are sturdier.

## What changed for users

- **A real approval workflow for Transport Requests.** Each request moves through
  clear steps with action buttons, the correct approver at each tier, segregation of
  duties (a requester cannot approve their own request), and — importantly — the steps
  after approval (Schedule, Fulfil) now actually work.
- **Reports you can slice.** Cost and fleet reports gain Project, Company and Cost
  Center filters, and the trend, aging and variance reports now show an inline chart.
- **Sturdier engines.** Fuel accrual no longer misses a request that is completed late,
  operational alerts clear themselves once their cause is resolved, and the nightly jobs
  run leaner.

## What changed under the hood

- Transport Request status is driven by a native Frappe Workflow (with a server-derived
  authority tier), replacing the hand-rolled status logic. Reliability improvements
  across the guest request form and the engine ledgers.

## If you are updating an existing site

Pull, then `bench migrate` and clear cache. The workflow and its states are installed
automatically; existing requests keep their status.
