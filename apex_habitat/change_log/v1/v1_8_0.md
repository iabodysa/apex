# Apex 1.8.0

More Movement documents now move through a real, native approval workflow.

## What changed for users

- **Rental Settlement approval flow.** Rental settlements move through Reconcile →
  Approve → Mark Paid, with a Finance-only "Mark Paid" step (only Finance can mark a
  settlement paid, and a person cannot approve their own request).
- **Driver Clearance workflow.** A driver can only be marked "Cleared" once fuel,
  custody and vehicle returns are settled — the workflow won't offer the step otherwise.
- **Post-approval steps work.** As with Transport Requests, the steps after a document
  is approved are now reachable, plus reliability improvements.

## What changed under the hood

- Rental Settlement and Driver Clearance now use native Frappe Workflows (states mapped
  to docstatus, role-gated transitions, segregation of duties), continuing the move off
  the hand-rolled status logic.

## If you are updating an existing site

Pull, then `bench migrate` and clear cache. The workflows install automatically; existing
documents keep their status.
