# Apex 1.5.0

Records are connected, Salis has a guided start, and navigation is cleaner.

## What changed for users

- **See related records on every form.** Open a vehicle, driver, custody article,
  maintenance request, lease and more, and the Connections tab now shows the documents
  linked to it — no more empty "Linked Documents".
- **A Salis getting-started guide.** A new "Salis Go-Live" journey walks a new site
  through its first vehicle category, vehicle, driver, assignment, dispatch and fuel
  request, right from the top of the Salis workspace.
- **Dashboards and charts from the workspace.** The Salis area workspaces now link
  straight to their dashboards, and the main Salis page shows trend charts at a glance.
  Area names are short and clear in the sidebar.
- **Email for the Movement team.** Optional, ready-to-enable email templates
  (driver clearance, rental settlement, transport acknowledgement) and scheduled email
  reports (fuel reconciliation, cost-recovery aging, fulfilment SLA, compliance).

## What changed under the hood

- Reliability and tidiness improvements.

## If you are updating an existing site

Pull, then `bench migrate` and clear cache. The migration tidies the Salis sidebar and
brings in the new connections, guide, dashboards and email options. No data migration
is required.
