# Apex 1.0.0

The big one. **Salis**, the Movement module, joins Habitat — so now you can manage
both where your workforce lives and how it moves, in one place. This is Apex: a
workforce-operations suite of two modules, and our first stable 1.0.0 release.

## What changed for users

- **Take command of Movement.** A whole new module for your fleet and workforce
  transport, with its own workspace. Operations asks, Movement delivers — across two
  service lines:
  - **Workers transport** — shuttle people from camp to project site (linked to your
    Habitat buildings and projects) and relocate the workforce between cities.
  - **Representatives** — administrative trips and document-signing runs, rider cars,
    fuel, and vehicle handover and return.
- **Approvals that route themselves.** Every fuel, transport and transfer request finds
  the right approver on its own — small ones clear fast, while bigger ones (cross-project,
  over-threshold, large inter-city moves, frequent admin trips) escalate up the chain
  automatically, and only the right person can sign them off.
- **Total fuel control.** Set quotas, raise and top up requests, track chips, watch a
  daily consumption ledger, reconcile each month, and settle any disputed fuel cleanly
  with an evidence-backed exception case. Clear pending requests in seconds from the
  **Fuel Approval Console**.
- **Rental and cost, end to end.** Receive and return rental vehicles, watch the daily
  rental cost add up, close the month with a single **Rental Settlement**, and hand
  finance a ready-to-pay request. Movement cost recovery and cost transfer are built in
  too — all as operational memos that reference your finance documents.
- **The Driver Portal, in every pocket.** A mobile-first web app at **/driver** where a
  driver checks in and out, sees today's trips, requests fuel, and raises a support
  ticket — each scoped to themselves. Switch it on from Salis Settings.
- **Compliance and safety, watched for you.** Track each vehicle's registration,
  insurance, inspection and operating card with expiry alerts; capture signed handover
  evidence; gate dispatch on readiness; and clear every driver before they roll out.
- **One view of each person and partner.** Open an **Employee** (HRMS) or **Supplier**
  (ERPNext) and their Movement story is right there — driver record, cost recovery,
  rental office — as quick reference links.

## What changed under the hood

- A new **Salis** module ships alongside Habitat (the package stays `apex_habitat`; the
  display title is now "Apex"). It spans masters, fleet, fuel, movement and support,
  plus system-written engine ledgers (rental accrual, fuel consumption, trip fulfilment,
  vehicle-utilisation snapshots) and a Movement Operations Dashboard.
- Movement records are scoped per project, kept on an append-only activity log linked to
  their source documents, and the tiered approvals are enforced on the server.
- Salis seeds its defaults safely on install, so a fresh site comes up ready to use.

## If you are updating an existing site

Run `bench migrate`, then `bench build` and clear cache after pulling. To use the Driver
Portal, enable it in Salis Settings and link each driver's Employee to a User with the
Driver role. This is the stable 1.0.0 release; the previous 0.9.1 line remains available
as a secondary option.
