# Apex 1.0.0-beta

The big one: **Salis**, the Movement Department module, joins Habitat (accommodation)
inside the app. The product is now "Apex" — a workforce-operations suite of two modules,
not just accommodation. Stable 0.9.1 is untouched; this is the beta development line.

## What changed for users

- **Salis — Movement Department.** A full module for fleet and workforce transport, with
  its own workspace, modelled on AFMCO governance as a *service provider*: Operations
  requests, Movement fulfils. Two divisions:
  - **Workers transport** — accommodation → project-site shuttle (linked to your Habitat
    buildings and projects) and inter-city workforce relocation.
  - **Representatives** — administrative trips and document-signing, rider cars, fuel/petrol,
    and vehicle handover/receipt.
- **Tiered approvals (Delegation of Authority).** Fuel, transport and transfer requests
  escalate by amount/quantity/scope through Supervisor → Project → Regional → Operations,
  and the approver must actually hold the required tier. Cross-project and over-threshold
  requests, inter-city > 20 workers, and admin trips > 5/month route upward automatically.
- **Fuel control.** Fuel quotas, requests, top-ups, chips, a daily consumption ledger,
  monthly reconciliation, and a **Fuel Exception Case** (evidence required, raiser ≠ closer)
  for disputed fuel. A **Fuel Approval Console** page approves pending requests inline.
- **Rental & cost.** Rental vehicle receipt/return, a daily rental accrual, monthly
  **Rental Settlement**, and a finance-exclusive **Payment Request** — all operational
  memos, **no GL postings** from the app. Movement Cost Recovery / Cost Transfer too.
- **Driver Portal.** A mobile-first web app at **/driver** where a driver checks in/out,
  sees today's trips, requests fuel, and raises support tickets — each scoped to themselves.
  Enable it from Salis Settings → "Enable Driver Portal".
- **Compliance & safety.** Per-vehicle compliance (registration/insurance/inspection/operating
  card) with expiry alerts; vehicle handover signed-evidence + discrepancy; dispatch-readiness
  gate; driver clearance before exit.
- **Official-app integration.** The **Employee** (HRMS) and **Supplier** (ERPNext) forms now
  surface the related Salis records (driver, cost recovery, rental office) as reference links;
  no accounting documents are written.

## What changed under the hood

- New `Salis` module (package stays `apex_habitat`; display title is now "Apex"). ~40 DocTypes
  across Masters, Fleet, Fuel, Movement, Governance/Support, plus system-written engine ledgers
  (Rental Accrual, Fuel Consumption, Trip Fulfilment, Vehicle Utilisation Snapshot) and a
  Movement Operations Dashboard.
- Core-name collisions avoided: Vehicle/Driver/Activity Log are prefixed (`Salis Vehicle`,
  `Salis Driver`, `Salis Activity Log`) so they never clash with ERPNext/HRMS/Frappe core.
- Project-scoped row permissions, an append-only Salis Activity Log linked to source records,
  and tiered approval enforced server-side.
- Salis defaults seed install-safe via `after_install` + fixtures + existence-guarded patches.

## If you are updating an existing site

Run `bench migrate`, then `bench build` and clear cache after pulling. To use the Driver
Portal, enable it in Salis Settings and link each driver's Employee to a User with the Driver
role. This is a beta line; the stable release remains 0.9.1.
