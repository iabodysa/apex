# Apex 1.14.2

A refreshed, mobile-first driver portal and a cleaner fuel approval board.

## What changed for users

- **A mobile-first driver portal.** The portal is redesigned for a phone: a clean,
  branded home screen showing your status, assigned vehicle and license at a glance — with
  the license turning amber, then red, as it nears or passes its expiry — plus icon-based
  navigation and large, tap-friendly action tiles.
- **A clearer Fuel Approval Console.** Each pending request now leads with the driver name
  and vehicle plate instead of an internal code, shows litres and amount prominently, and
  flags over-threshold requests — sorting them first — in a tidy, responsive card layout.
- **A friendlier staff landing.** Opening the portal as office staff now shows a neat card
  with quick links to your desk tools, rather than a plain list of links.

## What changed under the hood

- The driver portal ships a small inline-SVG icon set, so it needs no new icon dependency;
  the home, shell and staff screens were rebuilt with the existing brand palette.
- The Fuel Approval Console's styles are self-contained and theme-aware, and its rendering
  stays strictly text-based (no change to the workflow-driven approve/reject logic).

## If you are updating an existing site

Pull, rebuild assets and clear cache. Do one hard refresh of the driver portal in your
browser so it picks up the new version.
