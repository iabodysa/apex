# Apex 1.9.1

The Driver Portal now opens reliably for everyone.

## What changed for users

- **The driver home screen loads reliably.** A date-formatting issue in the portal's
  startup could blank the page (and show a misleading "not linked" message); it is fixed.
- **A useful screen for managers.** If you open the portal while signed in as staff (not a
  driver), you now see a friendly panel with quick links to your Salis areas — the Salis
  workspace and the Dispatch Board — instead of a dead-end.
- **Honest errors.** If the portal genuinely can't load, it now says so with a Retry
  button, rather than wrongly claiming your account isn't linked.

## What changed under the hood

- The portal bootstrap returns date fields as text so the response always serializes; the
  app distinguishes a real load error from the "no driver profile" state.

## If you are updating an existing site

Pull, rebuild the Driver Portal assets and clear cache. Tip: do a hard refresh in the
browser once, so the portal's offline cache picks up the new version.
