# Apex 1.26.0

The Salis approval documents now run on native Frappe Workflow — a single, standard
approval mechanism instead of a bespoke authority engine.

## What changed for users

- **One approval mechanism, not two.** Fuel Request, Fuel Claim, Fuel Exception
  Case, Movement Cost Recovery, Movement Cost Transfer and Vehicle Damage
  Write-Off are now approved entirely through their native Frappe Workflow. The
  same people who could approve before still approve, and no one can approve their
  own document.
- **New workflows for movement-cost and write-off documents.** Movement Cost
  Recovery, Movement Cost Transfer and Vehicle Damage Write-Off gained their own
  native approval workflows.

## What changed under the hood

- The custom authority-tier gate (`ensure_approval`) was removed from all six
  controllers; approval authority is expressed by each workflow's approve
  transition (allowed roles + `allow_self_approval = 0` + a maker-≠-approver
  condition). This removes the previous double-gating where a document was checked
  by both the tier engine and a workflow.
- Three new native Workflows were added and registered with the idempotent Salis
  workflow seed. Permission-layer segregation-of-duties hooks are unchanged.

## If you are updating an existing site

- Run `bench migrate`, then `bench build` and `bench clear-cache`. The new
  workflows are seeded automatically.
