# Apex 1.9.0

Three more Movement documents now move through a real, native approval workflow.

## What changed for users

- **Payment approval flow.** Salis Payment Requests go Draft → Submit to Finance →
  Approve (Finance) → Mark Paid. Only Finance approves and marks paid, and no one can
  approve their own request. (Movement still posts no accounting entries.)
- **Support Ticket workflow.** Tickets move through a clear status flow; staff resolve
  and close, and a driver still sees only their own tickets.
- **Sponsorship transfers.** A transfer can be completed only once Qiwa approval and the
  clearance step are done.
- **Post-approval steps work** across these documents, plus reliability improvements.

## What changed under the hood

- Salis Payment Request, Support Ticket and Sponsorship Transfer Case now use native
  Frappe Workflows (role-gated transitions, segregation of duties), continuing the move
  off the hand-rolled status logic.

## If you are updating an existing site

Pull, then `bench migrate` and clear cache. The workflows install automatically; existing
documents keep their status.
