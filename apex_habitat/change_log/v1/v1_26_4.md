# Apex 1.26.4

A tidier desk: fewer, more logical workspaces.

## What changed for users

- **"Facilities" workspace** — Maintenance (Upkeep) and Safety are now one
  workspace, since they are the same daily facility-upkeep surface.
- **"Movement" workspace** — Workers (transport) and Fuel are now one workspace,
  since dispatch/trips and the fuel that powers them are one operator view.
- **Fleet stays separate** — vehicles are a distinct asset surface, untouched.

## What changed under the hood

- Added the two merged Workspace definitions and re-pointed the Salis parent
  workspace; a guarded, idempotent patch removes the four old workspaces
  (Upkeep / Safety / Workers / Fuel) and loads the two new ones. No DocTypes,
  reports or pages changed — only navigation.

## If you are updating an existing site

- Run `bench migrate` (applies the workspace merge), then `bench clear-cache`.
