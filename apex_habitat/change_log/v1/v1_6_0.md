# Apex 1.6.0

The shared settings now live together in one place.

## What changed for users

- **One home for shared settings.** Habitat Settings and Salis Settings now sit under a
  new **Apex Core** area instead of being split across the two modules — easier to find,
  with no change to what they do or to your saved values.

## What changed under the hood

- Introduced an `Apex Core` module and re-homed the settings documents into it; document
  names and stored values are unchanged, so existing configuration is preserved.

## If you are updating an existing site

Pull, then `bench migrate` and clear cache. Your settings and their values carry over
automatically.
