# Apex 1.4.0

Movement costs now sit in your financial picture, and Salis is quicker to move around.

## What changed for users

- **Company and Cost Center on Movement records.** Rental settlements, fuel claims,
  payment requests and cost recoveries now carry a Company and Cost Center, set for you by
  default. Finance can filter and group Movement costs the same way they do everywhere else.
- **Reports you can slice by Company.** The rental-cost, fuel-spend, cost-recovery and
  payment reports gain a Company filter and column.
- **Shorter area names.** Salis areas read as Workers, Fleet, Fuel, Rentals, Compliance and
  Setup — clear in the sidebar, no truncation.
- **A Help-menu shortcut.** Jump to Salis and the Dispatch Board straight from the desk
  Help menu.

## What stayed the same

- Movement remains an operational record-keeper: it captures Company and Cost Center for
  reporting only and still posts no accounting entries — finance documents are produced on
  the finance side, exactly as before.

## If you are updating an existing site

Pull, then `bench migrate` and clear cache. Existing records keep working; the new Company
and Cost Center fields fill in with your defaults.
