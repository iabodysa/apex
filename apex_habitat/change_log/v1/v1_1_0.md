# Apex 1.1.0

Salis opens straight to the numbers that matter, on a cleaner two-division workspace,
and status colours now read at a glance across the whole app.

## What changed for users

- **See your fleet the moment Salis opens.** The workspace now leads with a Key Metrics
  row — active vehicles, vehicles in the shop, open transport requests and pending
  approvals — so you know where things stand before you click anything.
- **One workspace, two sides of Movement.** Salis is organised around its two service
  lines — a **Workers Transport** section and a **Representatives Fleet** section — so
  you go straight to the work you're doing.
- **Two dashboards, the full picture.** "Salis — Workers Transport" shows request status
  and type, transport over time, and demand by project; "Salis — Representatives Fleet"
  shows vehicles by status, fuel spend trends, rental accrual and dispatch trips.
- **Read status at a glance.** Vehicles, drivers, trips, fuel, maintenance and
  accommodation now wear colour-coded status everywhere you look — in lists, on Kanban
  and Calendar boards, and in reports.

## What changed under the hood

- Salis now ships its metric cards, charts and dashboards as part of the app, so they
  arrive with a migrate rather than appearing only at runtime.
- Status colours now come straight from each record's own status, so they stay
  consistent across every view.

## If you are updating an existing site

Pull, then `bench migrate` to bring in the new cards, charts, dashboards and status
colours, and clear cache. No data migration is required.
